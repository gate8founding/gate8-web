01 — WHAT

A racing term.
A mindset.
A movement.

In BMX, there are eight lanes. The inside lanes are the golden spots — the shortest line to the corner, the best chance to win. Then there's the last one. The outsider lane. The one nobody wants.

Gate8 is more than a BMX racing term. It's the idea that no matter where you start — a tough childhood, a setback, a relapse, a bad lane — you can still cross the line first.

Inspired by 9× BMX World Champion Shanaze Reade, Gate8 turns the toughest start into a universal symbol of resilience. You can win from anywhere.
