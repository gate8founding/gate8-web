2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to enable websites to function efficiently, improve user experience, and provide analytical information to site owners.

Cookies may be session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (which remain stored until deleted or expire automatically).
